How It Works

IQ provides internet service through fix based wireless internet technology also known as a WISP (Wireless Internet Service Provider). While many ISP's still insist on deploying miles of fiber that drives up your bill through surcharges and taxes, IQ's connection from the tower to your home is completely wireless; cutting cost by up to $40 per month.

So here is how it works. First, a technician will come to your residence and see if the signal will be good enough to provide the requested speeds at your location. If so, the technical will then Schudel an installation date where a small dish will be installed on your roof. This dish will communicate with our main tower and allow for you to surf the web just like a normal fiber line would. There will be some wires that will have to be run to the area where you want to put the router. Once the technician configures everything you will be set to enjoy reliable high-speed internet.

But whats the catch? Nothing really! This technology is currently being adopted many carriers including Google Fiber. Unlike most carriers, weather and high traffic loads play no factor in your connection speed at IQ.. IQ has built the network from the ground up meaning it is built to last.
